Hakeem Elliot is Harlem resident and graduate of New York University with a bachelor of the arts in Social Cultural Analysis. He started his career in public service, working for the National Development Council and The Center for Law and Social Jusitce. He as mentored numerous highscool and college students and is currently a college couch for Americorps. He is experianced in HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript coding languages. He also holds expertise in photoshop, photogropy, video directing, and is a creative director for Crown Jewel based in the Bronx. On his spare time you can catch him in your local museam or gaming, watching anime, or gaming on his playstation (the best system). Hakeem hopes to transition his full time into the tech and coding industry, while maintaining his passion for public service and creativity.
